# Welcome to Pacto Lúdico

Pacto Lúdico is a web-based platform designed for board game enthusiasts to explore, organize, and manage their gaming experiences. Whether you're a casual player or a community organizer, Pacto Lúdico provides tools to discover games, track events, and connect with fellow gamers.

## Browse by Category

Explore our comprehensive game categories:

- **Ameritrash** - Thematic games with high player interaction
- **Cards** - Card-based games from traditional to modern
- **Dice** - Games centered around dice mechanics
- **Eurogames** - Strategy-focused European-style board games
- **Miniatures** - Games featuring miniature figures
- **Party Games** - Social games for large groups
- **Role-playing** - RPGs and storytelling games
- **Traditional** - Classic board games
